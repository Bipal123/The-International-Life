About the Author

Hey!

Well how rude am I to not introduce myself first.

Well, I am a 19 year old kid from the mountainous region of Nepal; we got Mt. Everest (only the biggest mountain in the world) incase you ever do decide to visit us. Currently, I am a freshman at the University of Cincinnati studying Information Technology.

In a typical day where I don't have any assignment or studying to do, you would find me playing a video game with my friends (and, occasionally, childish players from the internet), reading a book while wrapped in a comfortable blanket on the bed, or watching some drama on Netflix. I also enjoy working out on the gym and, occasionally, go for swimming. If you love watching movies, then I could be your next best friend. I enjoy watching anything and everything from the Marvel Cinematic Universe to The Shawshank Redemption.

I also like to keep myself updated about the current world events, make new friends, and imagine my life in other countries.

I'm Bipal Goyal, and it is an absolute pleasure to meet you!
